Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Clashe respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you use Clashe.

2. Information Collected

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information such as username and email address.
  • Profile information you choose to add.
  • Content you post, including takes and comments.
  • Interactions such as follows, votes, bookmarks, and other engagement actions.
  • Device, browser, and usage information to operate and secure the service.

3. How Information Is Used

We use information to:

  • Operate and maintain the platform.
  • Personalize feeds and content experiences.
  • Improve product features and reliability.
  • Detect abuse, enforce policies, and maintain security.

4. Social Login Providers

If you sign in through social login providers (for example Google, Facebook, X, or other supported providers), those providers may share basic profile details with us, such as your name and email address, based on your provider settings and consent.

5. Data Storage

We store and process data using third-party infrastructure providers, including Supabase. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Data Sharing

Clashe does not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers that help us operate Clashe, or when required by law, legal process, or valid government request.

7. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, support platform functionality, and understand product usage through analytics.

8. User Rights

You can request account deletion and related data removal. You may also contact us to request access or correction of certain personal information, subject to applicable law.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make material updates, we will post the updated version on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact: privacy@clashe.app
